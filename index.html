MOHYAL EDUCATIONAL RESEARCH INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY

Mohyal Educational & Research Institute of Technology" acronym as MERIT was established in October '99 by GENERAL MOHYAL SABHA (Regd.) to set up an educational society with emphasis on Information Technology. Since then, the institution has been achieving newer heights by empowering the youth of both Mohyals and Non-Mohyals. Quality is the basis and Perfectionism is the ultimate goal in all our activities. Both are to be practiced on a continuous basis in a committed environment of teamwork that fosters innovation, leadership and expertise, as a result of value added education through self-learning process with hands-on training and online case studies. Youth are the building blocks of society and this MERIT knew from the beginning. Thus, its core functions were planned keeping in mind the following youth-oriented aspects.
